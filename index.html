FAQ

Honest answers to
real questions

Is there really no credit card required?+
Correct. The free plan is permanently free and needs no payment info. When you upgrade to Pro, you'll enter card details only then.
What happens after my free trial ends?+
If you don't cancel, you'll be charged for your chosen plan. If you cancel before the trial ends, you keep all your data and revert to the free plan — no charge, no questions asked.
How does the AI Coach actually work?+
The AI Coach is powered by Anthropic's Claude. It reads your real habit completion data, streak history, and focus session patterns to generate personalized insights. You can also ask it questions directly — it responds based on your actual data, not generic advice.
Can I export my data?+
Yes. All users can export their habit data as CSV. Annual plan users also get guaranteed lifetime data access and backup.
Is my data private and secure?+
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties. AI analysis is done server-side and not stored by the AI provider.
Can I use FocusFlow on my phone?+
Yes — FocusFlow is a fully responsive web app that works on all devices. Open it in Safari or Chrome on mobile and add it to your home screen for an app-like experience. Native iOS/Android apps are on the roadmap.
